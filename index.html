The Run

Hosts & Flow

Destiny Muse is hosting and running the flow. DJ Alphabass is on the decks throughout. You'll be onstage with the contestants — stay close, stay collaborative, stay fair. We'll prompt transitions and keep everything moving.

Competition Structure

The Rounds

Round 0 · 3 Minutes
Roll Time (no judging yet)

All participants get equal portions of weed and papers. They have 3 minutes to roll live — no pre-rolling. You can observe technique but nothing is scored here.

Round 1
Best Roll — Visual

Unlit assessment. Look at construction and finish.

  • Clean, tight roll
  • Even distribution of bud
  • No loose ends, rips, or weak structure
  • Looks durable and ready to smoke
Eliminations
Start with 6 → cut 2. Start with 10 → cut 4. Target: narrow to 4–6 finalists.
Round 2
Best Pull — Functionality

Remaining joints get lit and passed between judges. Take 1–2 smooth pulls and observe.

  • Smooth inhale
  • Even, steady burn
  • No clogging or struggle
  • Structural integrity once lit
Eliminations
Cut 2 more. We're down to the final 2.
Final Round
Longest Lit

Final two joints are lit at the same time and passed around. We're watching for which joint stays lit the longest without needing to be relit — and overall burn consistency.

Important
No excessive puffing to force it lit. Natural hits only. Let the roll do the work.
Tiebreaker
Speed Roll

If the final two joints both stay lit too long to call it:

  • Each finalist gets fresh materials
  • 1 minute to roll a new joint
  • We judge speed + structure + smokability
  • Fastest, cleanest roll wins
The Vibe

How to show up

  • Stay aligned and collaborative when you're deciding who advances
  • Judging should be fair, honest, and rooted in joint quality — no popularity contest
  • Keep it fun, respectful, and hype — this is about community, creativity & presence
  • Destiny will guide the flow and prompt transitions — follow her lead
Heads up — Mic Moments

Fun questions coming your way

Throughout the competition, Destiny will ask you a few fun questions to engage the crowd. Here's what to have ready so you can show up charming and prepared 💋

  1. When did you start smoking weed? What's the story?
  2. What's your go-to strain and why?
  3. How do you incorporate weed into your day-to-day life?
  4. What's the craziest thing someone has judged you about for smoking?
  5. What's your ideal smoke setting — alone, with friends, at a show, at home?
  6. Joint, blunt, or bong — and defend your answer.
  7. If your smoke habit had a theme song, what would it be?
  8. What's a misconception about weed culture you'd want to clear up?
  9. What's the weirdest place you've ever smoked?
  10. What's one weed accessory you can't live without?
No pressure
You won't get every one of these — they're prompts. Show up as yourself and have fun with it.
